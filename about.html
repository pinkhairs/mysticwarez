What is tech witchcraft?

Technology and magic might seem like they don't mix, but actually, technology can really help with witchcraft's focus on nature and spirit. Witches are practical, creative, and brave, making them great at using technology to make their magic even better. With things like digital spell books and online rituals, technology helps witches do more with their craft and improve their everyday lives.

What is Mysticwarez?

Mysticwarez is a collaborative space where people who are interested in both technology and spirituality can come to learn together with real tech projects.

Some of our projects 💫

We have easy-to-follow web development tutorials (with more domains like hardware being covered soon) that you can learn with.

Who should join?

If you're a creative person who likes to mix things up—like being a tattoo artist and a psychic or tarot reader at the same time—you'll fit right in here. We love people who enjoy trying new things, making practical things by hand, and care deeply about being responsible, ethical, and inclusive. If you're the kind of person who is dedicated to being fair and making a positive impact, our platform is perfect for you.
